PATRICK FIORE, SR. PROJECT MANAGER
Patrick is a United States Navy Veteran with thirty years of construction experience in handling an array of commercial and residential projects. He is an exceptionally skilled general contractor with additional expertise in bidding and obtaining jobs. His comprehension of cost estimation, renovations, and new projects is second to none. With a proven ability to manage subcontractors, laborers, and all materials necessary for the job, he brings a focused and attentive presence to every minor and major construction need. He is praised by owners for delivering projects within deadlines and maintaining strong communication skills.
